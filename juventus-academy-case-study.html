Juventus Academy App Case Study Proposal

The Juventus Academy seeks to improve communication and streamline administrative processes by developing a dedicated app. This app will enhance the interaction between parents and the academy by managing player profiles, tracking payments, and handling event scheduling. The goal is to create a user-friendly experience for parents.

Problem Statement

Currently, the Juventus Academy relies on WhatsApp groups for communication and paper forms for registration, leading to several issues:

Pain Points

  • Communication Overload: Important information is scattered across multiple WhatsApp groups.
  • Inefficient Registration: Paper forms are cumbersome, prone to errors, and cause delays.
  • Lack of Centralized Information: Parents have difficulty accessing and tracking their child's profile, events, and payment status.

Design Process

  • Research and Discovery:Conduct interviews with parents to understand their challenges with the current system and identify their needs.
  • Define Requirements:Determine essential features for the app, including registration, profile management, event tracking, and payment status.
  • Design:Create high-level wireframes for the parent interface.Develop interactive prototypes to illustrate user flows and key functionalities.
  • Validation:Test prototypes with a sample of parents to gather feedback and make necessary refinements.

Key Features

  • Step-by-Step Registration: Streamlined onboarding process for new players.
  • Home Screen Overview: Centralized access to player profiles, upcoming events, payment status, and notifications./li>
  • Event Management: Ability to view and register for events and sessions.
  • Payment Tracking: Real-time updates on payment status and history
  • Notifications: Timely updates on announcements, new events, and coach changes.
